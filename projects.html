PROJECTS

Maharagama General Hospital Water Filter Project

We are proud to support Maharagama General Hospital with the installation of state of the art water filtration systems. This project ensures patients and staff have access to clean and safe drinking water, supporting better health and hygiene within the hospital.

Project Details

Location: Maharagama General Hospital, Sri Lanka

Completion Date: November 2024

System Installed: PureHydro BlueWave Purifier Commercial System

Capacity: 1,200L/day, serving 500+ people daily

Colombo Public School Initiative

Installation of water purification systems in 10 public schools across Colombo district, providing clean drinking water to over 5,000 students and staff members. This initiative focuses on ensuring children have access to safe water during school hours.

Project Impact

Schools Covered: 10 public schools

Beneficiaries: 5,000+ students and staff

System Installed: KENT GRAND PLUS RO+UV Systems

Completion: Ongoing, Phase 2 in progress
